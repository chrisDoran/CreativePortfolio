Politician Sam

Creative Director

Virtual Politician Project

Politician Sam is a project aiming to create a virtual politician for a future NZ election. By using a natural language conversation system and machine learning techniques, Sam can chat to and learn from users across most social media sites.

As creative director I worked alongside a team of designers and developers to develope the technology for Sam to function as well as creating a recognisable brand. The project was featured at New Zealands D5 technology summit, allowing key members of New Zealands pariament, including Jacinda Ardern, a chance to test our technology.
